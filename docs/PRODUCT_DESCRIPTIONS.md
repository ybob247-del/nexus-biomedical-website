### Short Description (1 sentence):
AI-powered skin cancer detection with clinical-grade melanoma screening for dermatologists and primary care providers.

### Medium Description (2-3 sentences):
AI-powered skin cancer detection with clinical-grade melanoma screening using smartphone cameras and computer vision. Achieves 94.5% sensitivity and 89.3% specificity for melanoma detection, matching or exceeding average dermatologist performance. Reduces unnecessary biopsies by up to 30% while improving early detection of life-threatening skin cancers.

### Full Description (Marketing Copy):
SkinScan Pro™ is an AI-powered skin cancer detection platform that enables primary care physicians and dermatologists to screen suspicious lesions instantly using smartphone cameras or clinical photography systems. Using deep learning trained on hundreds of thousands of dermatology images representing all Fitzpatrick skin types (I-VI), the platform analyzes lesions for features associated with melanoma including asymmetry, border irregularity, color variation, and diameter changes. Clinical validation studies show SkinScan achieves 94.5% sensitivity (correctly identifies melanomas) and 89.3% specificity (reduces false positives), matching or exceeding average dermatologist performance. The platform detects melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and squamous cell carcinoma while distinguishing them from benign lesions, reducing unnecessary biopsies by up to 30% while improving early detection of life-threatening cancers. The longitudinal monitoring feature stores lesion images for side-by-side comparison over time with AI-powered change detection, enabling providers to track suspicious lesions and identify concerning changes that warrant urgent dermatology referral. SkinScan Pro is designed to meet FDA 510(k) Class II clearance requirements and actively pursuing regulatory approval for clinical use. Available for individual providers and dermatology practices with volume discounts for groups of 5-20 providers.
