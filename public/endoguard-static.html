What is EndoGuard™?

EndoGuard™ is an AI-powered endoscopy quality assurance and adverse event prediction platform. It helps gastroenterologists and endoscopy centers monitor procedure quality, predict potential complications, and improve patient safety through advanced analytics.

Key Features

  • Quality metrics tracking for endoscopic procedures
  • Adverse event prediction using AI algorithms
  • Procedure analytics and performance dashboards
  • Compliance reporting for quality standards
  • Risk assessment for individual patients
  • Real-time alerts for high-risk procedures

Pricing

  • Professional: $97/month - Quality tracking, adverse event prediction, analytics dashboard, 30-day free trial
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing - Multi-site support, API access, dedicated support, custom reporting

How It Works

  1. Patient Assessment: Complete a 6-step assessment covering patient history, procedure details, and risk factors
  2. Risk Analysis: AI analyzes patient data and procedure characteristics to calculate adverse event risk
  3. Risk Score: Receive a comprehensive risk score (0-100) with color-coded severity indicators
  4. Recommendations: Get personalized recommendations to minimize complication risk
  5. Quality Tracking: Monitor procedure quality metrics over time
  6. Compliance Reporting: Generate reports for quality assurance and regulatory compliance

Clinical Evidence

EndoGuard™'s AI models are trained on clinical research and real-world endoscopy data. The platform helps identify patients at higher risk for:

  • Post-procedure bleeding
  • Perforation
  • Infection
  • Adverse reactions to sedation
  • Incomplete procedures

HIPAA and Security

EndoGuard™ is HIPAA-ready with enterprise-grade security including AES-256 encryption, secure cloud storage, and comprehensive audit logging. As a pseudonymous platform that does not collect PHI (Protected Health Information) in standard use, EndoGuard™ does not require Business Associate Agreements for basic quality tracking.
