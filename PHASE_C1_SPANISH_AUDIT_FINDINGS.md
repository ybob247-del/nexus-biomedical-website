## FINDINGS BY PAGE

### PAGE 1: Spanish Homepage (/es)

#### Finding 1: Diagnostic Claim - EndoGuard Description
**URL:** https://www.nexusbiomedical.ai/es  
**Section:** Platform Cards - EndoGuard™  
**Exact Spanish Phrase Found:**
> "Evaluación de riesgo de endometriosis - una condición que afecta desproporcionadamente a mujeres hispanas"
> 
> "Detección temprana puede reducir años de diagnóstico"

**Why it Violates A3 Standards:**
- "Detección temprana" (early detection) is a diagnostic claim that implies the tool can detect endometriosis
- The phrase "puede reducir años de diagnóstico" (can reduce years of diagnosis) implies the tool has diagnostic authority
- EndoGuard is a risk stratification tool, NOT a diagnostic tool
- This language overstates the product's capability and violates the prohibition on diagnostic claims

**Recommended Compliant Spanish Replacement:**
> "Evaluación de riesgo de salud hormonal - para mujeres que desean entender mejor sus síntomas"
> 
> "Proporciona información para facilitar conversaciones con su proveedor de salud"

## AUDIT METHODOLOGY

This audit compared Spanish text against the following A3 compliance standards:

1. **No Diagnostic Claims:** Language that implies the tool can detect, identify, or diagnose medical conditions
2. **No Prediction Guarantees:** Language that implies the tool can prevent, reduce, or guarantee health outcomes
3. **No Treatment Authority:** Language that implies the tool can treat, cure, or manage medical conditions
4. **No Unsupported Clinical-Grade Claims:** Language that makes clinical claims without validation

All findings were identified by comparing Spanish text to the English version and evaluating against these standards.
