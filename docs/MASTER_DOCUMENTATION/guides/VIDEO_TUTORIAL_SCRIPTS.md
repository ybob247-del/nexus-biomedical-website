## Production Guidelines

### Video Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1920Ã—1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio:** 48kHz, stereo, AAC codec
- **Bitrate:** 5-8 Mbps for video, 192 kbps for audio

### Branding Requirements
- **Intro/Outro:** Use Nexus Biomedical Intelligence logo with cyan/purple gradient background
- **Font:** Use platform fonts (Inter, Roboto, or similar sans-serif)
- **Colors:** Cyan (#06B6D4), Purple (#A855F7), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Background Music:** Subtle, non-intrusive instrumental (royalty-free)
- **Narration:** Professional voice talent, clear enunciation, moderate pace

### Accessibility
- **Closed Captions:** Include accurate captions for all narration
- **Visual Descriptions:** Describe all on-screen actions in narration
- **Contrast:** Ensure text overlays have sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- **Pace:** Allow 2-3 seconds for viewers to read on-screen text before advancing

- **Platform Website:** Embed in help/tutorial sections
- **YouTube:** Upload to Nexus Biomedical Intelligence channel
- **Social Media:** Create 60-second highlight clips for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn
