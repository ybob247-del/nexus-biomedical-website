## Email Best Practices

1. **Keep it short** - 5 sentences or less
2. **One clear call-to-action** - Schedule a call, review proposal, etc.
3. **Personalize** - Reference specific pain points from your conversation
4. **Follow up** - 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks, then close
5. **Use Calendly** - Make scheduling easy
6. **Mobile-friendly** - Most people read on phone
7. **Subject lines** - Clear, specific, not salesy
8. **Signature** - Include phone, email, Calendly link
