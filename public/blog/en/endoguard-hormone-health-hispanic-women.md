## Common Hormone Conditions in Hispanic Women

### Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS)

PCOS affects 1 in 10 women of reproductive age. Symptoms include:

- Irregular or absent periods
- Excess facial and body hair
- Acne and oily skin
- Weight gain and difficulty losing weight
- Dark skin patches (acanthosis nigricans)

**How EndoGuard‚Ñ¢ helps**: Tracks symptoms, identifies patterns, and provides recommendations on lifestyle changes and when to seek medical care.

### Thyroid Problems

Thyroid disorders are more common in women, especially:

- **Hypothyroidism**: Underactive thyroid
- **Hyperthyroidism**: Overactive thyroid
- **Hashimoto's thyroiditis**: Autoimmune disease

**Common symptoms**:
- Extreme fatigue
- Unexplained weight changes
- Sensitivity to cold or heat
- Mood changes
- Concentration problems

**How EndoGuard‚Ñ¢ helps**: Monitors thyroid symptoms, suggests appropriate lab tests, and helps track treatment effectiveness.

### Perimenopause and Menopause

The transition to menopause can begin in your 40s, with symptoms like:

- Hot flashes and night sweats
- Mood swings and irritability
- Sleep problems
- Vaginal dryness
- Changes in libido

**How EndoGuard‚Ñ¢ helps**: Provides tracking of perimenopausal symptoms, education about treatment options, and support during this natural transition.
