# Competitive Pricing Analysis

Based on market research of actual competitors, here's what customers are paying for similar solutions:

---

## 1. RxGuard™ - Drug Interaction Checker

### Competitors:
- **Drug Interaction Checker apps**: $5-$10 one-time or $20-$50/year
- **Lexicomp (UpToDate)**: ~$500-$1,000/year for professionals
- **PEPID Pharmacist Pro**: ~$200-$400/year
- **Consumer apps**: $5-$10 (App Store)

### Market Pricing Range:
- **Consumer**: $5-$50/year
- **Professional**: $200-$1,000/year
- **Enterprise (EHR integration)**: $10,000-$100,000/year

### **RECOMMENDED PRICING:**
| Tier | Price | Rationale |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | Lead generation, limited use |
| **Professional** | **$39/month ($468/year)** | ✅ **Mid-market sweet spot** - more features than consumer apps, cheaper than Lexicomp |
| Enterprise | Custom ($10K-$50K/year) | EHR integration, unlimited users |

**Verdict:** ✅ **$39/month is competitive and well-positioned**

## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **RxGuard™ pricing is perfect** - $39/month is competitive
2. **ReguReady™ & ClinicalIQ™ need lower entry tiers** - $299/month for startups
3. **ElderWatch™ is overpriced** - Should be $49-$199, not $200-$500
4. **PediCalc™ is MASSIVELY overpriced** - Should be $9.99, not $99
5. **SkinScan™ is overpriced** - Should be $49, not $99

**Rule of thumb:** 
- **< $50/month** = Consumer/individual professional
- **$50-$500/month** = Small business/practice
- **> $500/month** = Enterprise (Contact Sales)
